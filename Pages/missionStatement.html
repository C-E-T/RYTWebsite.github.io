Mission Statement

Mission Statement: Our mission is to allow Windsor's youth a safe and welcoming environment to participate in the production of live theatre both onstage and behind the scenes. Our program is free of charge and open to all youth between the ages of 8 and 21 who are interested in any aspects of live performance. Revolution Youth is a non-secular company open to ALL youth with a desire to be part of the theatre community regardless of financial status, race, gender/gender identity, sexual orientation or religious denomination.
